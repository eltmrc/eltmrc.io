Most security bugs are confused deputies: something trusted did something it shouldn’t.

Draw the boundary. Name who is inside. Limit the tools that cross it.

![User, agent, world](/images/diagrams/trust.svg)

Agents make this sharper — capability without clear scope is just risk with a UI.
