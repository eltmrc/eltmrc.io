Users don’t measure p99. They measure *does this feel alive?*

Skeleton screens, optimistic UI, and small payloads often beat a “correct” spinner.

![Perceived speed vs feature surface](/images/diagrams/perf.svg)

## Rule of thumb

If something takes more than ~200ms, animate the wait. If it takes more than 2s, rethink the path.
