This is the home base for my writing.

I wanted a place that isn't the product marketing site — something slower and more personal. Notes on technologies I try, the story of building [Cial](https://cial.ai), and essays that don't need a changelog.

## What you'll find here

- **Essays** on product and systems
- **Lab notes** when I try a tool or stack hard enough to have an opinion
- **Field notes** from shipping in public, imperfectly

The design goal is simple: minimalist, polished, readable. No database — each post is a file in the repo, so the site stays as durable as git.
