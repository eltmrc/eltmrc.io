Launch demos are optimized for wow. Real evaluation needs a week of messy work.

I pick one real project, force the model through tools, and note where it fails: long context, tool use, taste, recovery.

![Agent in the middle of real work](/images/diagrams/trust.svg)

If it only shines in a chat toy, it doesn’t change how I build.
