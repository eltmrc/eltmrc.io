Early company calendars expand like gas. I treat maker blocks as product infrastructure.

## Weekly shape

- Mornings: build  
- Afternoons: people / decisions  
- One deep day with almost no meetings  

![Ship loop as calendar rhythm](/images/diagrams/loop.svg)

If the calendar doesn’t protect shipping, the product won’t either.
