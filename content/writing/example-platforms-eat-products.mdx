Products solve a job. Platforms host many jobs.

The risk: building a product on rented land where the platform eventually wants your margin.

![Stacked layers of a system](/images/diagrams/layers.svg)

Cial’s bet is ownership of the workspace layer — closer to a platform you control than a single chat feature.
