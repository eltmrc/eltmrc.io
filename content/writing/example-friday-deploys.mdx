“Never deploy on Friday” is a culture smell more often than a law.

If you can roll forward or back cleanly, Friday is fine. If deploy is a prayer, no day is safe.

![Ship as a closed loop](/images/diagrams/loop.svg)

Invest in the loop until the day of week stops mattering.
