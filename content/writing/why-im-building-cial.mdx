Most AI products still feel like a tab you visit, not a place you work.

I started Cial because I wanted something different: a **workspace** you own. Real login. Tools. Agents that can act on your machine. A product surface that survives model hype cycles.

## The bet

The long game isn't "which model is smartest this month." It's **who controls the environment** where work happens — sessions, memory, tools, automations, identity.

Cial is that environment: self-hosted, extensible, built for people who want the power of agents without renting their entire workflow to a black box SaaS.

## What I'm optimizing for

- **Ownership** — your instance, your data boundary, your customizations
- **Craft** — software that feels intentional, not bolted-on demos
- **Agency** — tools that actually do things, not just talk about them

This site is where I'll write about that journey: technical choices, wrong turns, and the quieter product decisions that don't fit in a launch tweet.

If you're building in the same space — or just curious — say hello.
